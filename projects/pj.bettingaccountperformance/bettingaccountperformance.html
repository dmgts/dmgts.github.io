Betting Account Performance Overview

1. Project Overview

Within the scope of my day to day responsibilities, this project entailed the transformation of PDF reports detailing account wagering histories into a format conducive to Excel-based analysis. The process involved selecting only the pertinent data, which was then cleansed, structured, and verified to guarantee its precision and utility for subsequent analytical purposes.

2. Data Processing Steps

During the data processing phase of the project, several key steps were undertaken to refine and structure the dataset for in-depth analysis, including:

  • Datetime Column Segregation:

    The project required the segregation of the combined Datetime column into distinct Date and Time columns to facilitate more nuanced analysis.

  • Column Label Refinement:

    For enhanced interpretability, modifications were made to column labels.

  • Odds Data Extraction and Standardization:

    The odds data were extracted from the selection column and standardized to ensure consistency across the dataset.

  • Selection Column Purification:

    A comprehensive purge of the selection column was undertaken to eliminate extraneous information.

  • Data Standardization via 'Find and Replace':

    This method was employed to unify the bet_type and result columns, thereby ensuring data uniformity.

  • Market Data Extraction and Cleansing:

    Market-related information was meticulously extracted and cleansed from the event column to maintain data integrity.

  • Profit and Loss (P/L) Computation:

    A new P/L column was introduced, enabling the direct assessment of financial performance.

  • Live Bet Designation

    A specific column was allocated to accurately indicate live betting instances, utilizing insights from the selection column.

  • Data Integrity Completion:

    Efforts were made to identify and rectify any data omissions, ensuring the dataset's comprehensiveness and reliability.

  • Odds Buckets Creation:

    Developed an odds buckets column, grouping bets into five categories based on their odds, to analyze patterns and outcomes more effectively.

  • Market Category Classification:

    Added a market category column, categorizing bets according to the market they belong to, providing insights into market-specific betting trends and behaviors.

3. Analysis and Metrics Calculation

Utilizing pivot table functionalities, critical metrics were computed to evaluate the betting account's strategic effectiveness and financial health, including:

  1. Return on Investment (ROI): Evaluates the profitability of betting activities.
  2. Picks Volume: Reflects the frequency of betting activity
  3. Hit Rate: Measures the precision of bet selections.
  4. Profit/Loss (P/L): Financial summary indicating net outcomes.
  5. Average Stake: Reveals the standard bet size, indicating the risk tolerance.
  6. Average Odds: Offers insights into the risk-return profile.
